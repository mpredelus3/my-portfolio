About Me

Hey there! I'm Mack Predelus. I was born and raised in the vibrant city of Miami, Florida, I've always been driven by a sense of purpose and a hunger for new challenges. After high school, I decided to serve my country, spending six impactful years as a Jet Engine Mechanic in the Air Force Reserves. Following my service, I pursued my passion for knowledge, earning a Bachelor's degree in Exercise Science from the University of West Florida in 2017. Currently, I'm diving headfirst into the world of coding at the University of Denver's Full-Stack Coding Bootcamp. It's an exhilarating journey, filled with late-night coding sessions, problem-solving puzzles, and the joy of seeing lines of code come to life. During the day, you'll find me wearing my AT&T hat as an In-Home Expert, where I get to connect with people everyday. It's a role that keeps me grounded while I work towards my career in technology.
